About the Course

Welcome to the DATA602 Principles of Data Science course. This course will cover essential topics in data science, including data collection, processing, analysis, and visualization. Students will gain hands-on experience with tools like Python, R, and SQL, and explore statistical methods, machine learning algorithms, and data-driven decision-making techniques.

Course Objectives:

  • Understand the fundamental principles of data science and its applications.
  • Learn how to clean, process, and visualize data effectively.
  • Apply statistical methods and machine learning techniques to solve real-world problems.
  • Develop critical thinking skills to approach data-driven challenges.

This course is designed for both beginners and those with some experience in data science. By the end of the semester, you'll have a solid foundation in data science principles and be well-prepared to tackle more advanced topics in the field.
